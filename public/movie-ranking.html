The Best Movies According to Alex

My top 3 movies of all time (so far):

Scott Pilgrim vs. The World

This isn't a movie for everyone, but if you are into retro gaming and enjoy 2000's parody type comedy, this movie will become a favorite.

The Matrix

Cinema at it's best. One of the movies of all time.

The Hateful Eight

The plot in this one will keep you guessing until the very end.
